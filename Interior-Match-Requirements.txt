

# ✅ FINAL MASTER PROMPT

**Subject: Premium Animated Interior Lead Website (High-Performance Motion UI, Bilingual EN/Tamil, Conversion-Focused)**

---

## Objective

Build a minimalist, premium landing website for an interior design lead-connection business that connects:

• **Clients who need interior work**
• **Interior builders/designers who want client leads**

The site must support **two languages**:
• English
• Tamil (தமிழ்)

with a visible **language toggle (EN / தமிழ்)** in the header.

The website should feel **luxurious, warm, and trustworthy**, while featuring **high-performance motion and liquid interactivity** inspired by modern design agency websites (reference style: string-tune.fiddle.digital), adapted for an **interior design business**.

Primary goals:
• Collect client requirements
• Collect builder partnership requests
• Provide WhatsApp and form-based contact
• Present a premium interior brand
• Feel smooth, elegant, and modern

---

## 1. Performance & Architecture

Priority:
• Zero layout shifts
• Sub-100ms interaction latency
• Lazy-load animations only when elements enter the viewport

Architecture:
• Modular section-based layout
• Animations triggered via HTML data attributes (e.g., `data-animate="fade-up"`, `data-animate="split-text"`)
• Avoid heavy continuous JS loops
• Language switching must not cause layout jumps

---

## 2. Motion & Interaction System

### Scroll & Reveal

• Refined smooth scrolling without breaking accessibility
• Sections reveal with staggered fades and subtle Y-axis translations
• Images reveal using clip-path or mask animations
• Gallery images softly zoom and parallax on scroll

### Magnetic Interactivity

• Buttons and links have magnetic pull toward cursor (40–50px radius)
• Hover states feel soft and premium

### Kinetic Typography

• Hero headline and section titles use split-text animation (line-by-line or word-by-word)
• Text reveals feel elegant and calm

### Microinteractions

• Liquid hover buttons
• Card lift with soft shadow
• Input focus animations
• CTA buttons pulse subtly

---

## 3. Design Aesthetics (Interior Theme)

### Color & Mood

• Light luxury palette: warm beige, off-white, sand, taupe, soft gray
• Accent: muted gold or bronze

### Texture

• Subtle animated grain/noise overlay
• Optional glassmorphism header

### Typography

• Premium sans-serif for English (Inter, Outfit, DM Sans)
• Tamil-compatible font (Noto Sans Tamil or Catamaran)
• Tight letter spacing
• Large hero text

### Layout

• Generous whitespace
• Full-width hero
• Editorial spacing
• Smooth section transitions
